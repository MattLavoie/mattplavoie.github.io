Tuesday, April 19th 2016 from 7 to 8:30 pm

Downtown UX Meetup

UX Advocacy - Buy in and Beyond

We all want to leverage UX Design in our process at work, but often we don't feel that it is a luxury we can afford. Or worse, we are not able to convince those we work with why it is so worth investing in.

In this talk we will discuss the challenges with championing for UX Design, why the data doesn't seem to make a difference, and how you can approach trying to convince your boss and your peers. We will dive into the benefits you get from buy in, why it's worth doing, and how to approach going beyond by creating a culture that values human centered design at its core.
